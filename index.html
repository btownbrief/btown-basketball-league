Btown Basketball League

Burlington's $20 Wednesday night run.

The League

A simple, low-cost ($20) basketball league built around Wednesday nights, balanced teams, and good full-court runs.

The Vision

This is a relatively serious pickup league for players who already know how to hoop, so those on the sidelines stay entertained.

📅

The Basics

Starts Wednesday April 22nd. 6-week season. Thursday rain backup.

📍

The Courts

Wednesday nights, 2 courts. Outdoor first / indoor backup space.

THE VIBE

More than just one weekly game.

🏆

The Goal

A competitive run with standings, a prize to play for, and a groupchat that helps people organize pickup runs across Burlington between league nights.

🍻

Social Atmosphere

I want a space where people can hang out, bring friends or family, and enjoy a good night around the court, bring food and all. The idea is to build a league night people want to stick around for, not just show up, play, and leave.

The Prize

The winning team gets a pizza party and free t-shirts. There is an actual prize at the end, so the games still mean something.

Rain Backup

Part of where your fees are going is toward renting an indoor space on rainy days when needed.

TEAMS & SIGNUP

Secure your spot or just hang out.

Balanced Teams

Players sign up individually and rate their own skill level from 1 to 10 so teams can be built as evenly as possible.

7-Player Max

7-player max rosters. Smaller teams mean better run and more playing time.

📝

Want In? Player Form

Fill out the signup form to register, rate your skill level 1-10 and send $20 to @BtownBrief on Venmo. This league is not designed for beginners still learning, we want runs that people on the sidelines want to see.

 💬

Join Pickup Chat

Can't commit? Join a citywide groupchat for organizing pickup runs across Burlington. Stay updated on the league and find games throughout the week for more laid-back runs.

 📰

Btown Brief

I have a Burlington newsletter with 3k subcribers looking for things to do around town. Give them a reason to come out.

THE RULES

Keep it moving, keep it clean.

Schedule 4-week regular season
Playoffs 2-week playoffs
Format Full-court 5v5
Clock Under 4 minutes, clock stoppage
Fouls Offense calls fouls
Free Throws One-shot free throw per shooting foul
Conduct No rough fouls
Contests Contested calls: shoot for it
Subs Everyone sits once before someone sits twice
Timekeeping Sub helps keep time
Tap edges or swipe to navigate
